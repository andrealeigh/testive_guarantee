If you put in the work, your score will go up. We guarantee it.

Details, Please.

We don’t believe in fine print. At the beginning of the program, you will make a commitment to put forth effort. We will make a commitment to provide you with great quality material and amazing coaching support.

What you commit to in a Testive program:

Advisor:

  • Complete all lesson practice sets and watch all video lessons
  • Complete a minimum of 100 practice questions each week spread over at least 3 days per week
  • Complete at least one full-length practice test each month

Coaching & Coaching Plus:

  • Meet with your coach weekly (or twice a week for Coaching Plus)
  • Complete a minimum of 100 practice questions each week spread over at least 3 days per week
  • Complete at least one full-length practice test each month

How We Measure Score Improvement:

We compare your total “before” score to your total “after” score.

  • “Before” score: If you have an official score, great! If not, that’s okay. We’ll just use the score from your first practice test with us as your baseline for measuring score improvement.
  • “After” score: This will need to be an official SAT or ACT score from the test that immediately follows your Testive program.

If we both do what we’ve promised, your score will go up. If we fail to deliver on our end, we will refund your money in full.
